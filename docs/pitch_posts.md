# VERBA - Pitch & Social Posts

---

## One-Line Pitch

> VERBA is a dating app where you connect through words first. No swiping on photos. Photos stay hidden until both people agree to reveal them. Words before looks.

---

## Extended Pitch

 Dating apps have become superficial - swiping on photos, judgment in seconds, pressure to look perfect.

VERBA is different:

- **No photos to swipe on** - Browse profiles without seeing photos
- **Connect through conversation** - Get to know someone before judging looks
- **Photos by mutual consent** - Only reveal when BOTH agree
- **True privacy** - No online status, no read receipts
- **One chat at a time** - Quality over quantity

We're building dating for introverts who want meaningful conversations, not superficial matches.
