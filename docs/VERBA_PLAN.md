# VERBA - Project Plan & Roadmap

## Last Updated: 2026-03-02

---

## Overview

**Verba** — Dating app for introverts
- **Tagline:** "Words before looks"
- **Core Concept:** Photos stay hidden until BOTH people agree to reveal them

---
